India is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area, the second-most populous country!

china

China is a country in East Asia. It is the world's most populous country, with a population of more than 1.4 billion!

maldives

The Maldives is a nation of islands in the Indian Ocean, that spans across the equator!

singapore

Singapore, is known as the Lion City and consists of the main island and about 64 smaller offshore islands!

japan

Japan is an island country in East Asia. It is situated in the northwest Pacific Ocean, and is bordered on the west by the Sea of Japan!

thailands

Thailand is a Southeast Asian country. It's known for tropical beaches, opulent royal palaces, ancient ruins and ornate temples displaying figures of Buddha!

bangladesh

Bangladesh, to the east of India on the Bay of Bengal, is a South Asian country marked by lush greenery and many waterways. Its Padma (Ganges), Meghna and Jamuna rivers create fertile plains, and travel by boat is common!

philippines

Philippines, island country of Southeast Asia in the western Pacific Ocean.The second largest island of the Philippines is Mindanao, in the southeast!

united arab emirates

The United Arab Emirates is a country in Western Asia. It is located at the eastern end of the Arabian Peninsula, and shares borders with Oman and Saudi Arabia!
